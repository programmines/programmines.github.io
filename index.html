Veg'Fest Online

Participation in Veg'Fest Morocco online is open to the public free of charge on the facebook page and on Instagram. This first digital edition is essential as a solution to curb the covid-19 pandemic.

Speakers & Guests

Among the speakers, this edition will include Doctors, nutritionists, ecologists, activists, representatives of associations working for the preservation of the environment and / or for the welfare of animals, researchers, philosophers and essayists from various origins and countries: Morocco, Tunisia, Zimbabwe, Rwanda, Guinea, Lebanon, Palestine, Iran, Spain, France, Switzerland, the United States, Canada, Poland, Germany and the United Kingdom.
