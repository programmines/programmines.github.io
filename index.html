Sponsors

The vegfest Morocco is the first vegan festival organized in North Africa by the Moroccan association Veg'Morocco.

Given its international reputation, sponsoring it is a great way to market your brand and make it more visible to a responsible audience.

We will promote your brand as sponsors and ensure that it is visible on our various media.

Do not hesitate to contact us on vegmorocco@gmail.com to find out more.
